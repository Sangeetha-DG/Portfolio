Description:

  • This project explores Food Stamp (SNAP) participation trends in New Jersey for the year 2022 using interactive visualizations built with Tableau.

  • The analysis focuses on uncovering patterns in food assistance distribution based on key socio-economic factors.

    • Key components of the dashboard include:

  • Overall SNAP Participation: About 8.6% of NJ households received food stamps.

  • By Poverty Level: 85% of households below poverty received assistance vs. only 14% above poverty.

  • By Race/Ethnicity: Hispanic households had the highest participation rates.

  • By Work Status: 43% of single-worker households continued to depend on SNAP benefits despite having employment.
